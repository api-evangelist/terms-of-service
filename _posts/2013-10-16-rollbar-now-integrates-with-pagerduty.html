Rollbar monitors application errors at the code level, aggregating similar issues and helping developers find patterns and fix problems faster.Rollbar has integrated their service to provide their customers methods for alerting, on-call scheduling and incident management to their system.The integration allows Rollbar customers to create, update and resolve incidents in PagerDuty.Richard Lee, CTO at Polydice, a mobile development studio focusing on iOS app design and development, uses both tools to make sure their clients’ products remain functional at all times.“With Rollbar’s integration of PagerDuty, we’re able to get notified as soon as errors detected, and avoid possible downtime to our customers.
