Think of social media as a repository of user data that is just waiting to be tapped.As a business, accessing this data will help you deliver products and services that are more attuned to the needs of your consumers.One of the ways you can leverage social media data for your business’s benefit is by integrating your web presence with social media.A social media friendly website is best placed to help your business experience the slew of benefits that social media can bring to the table.Businesses employ different techniques to merge social with their websites—while blogs and social share buttons are commonly used to improve the shareability of the site content, custom social widgets are also used to add customers to a business’s social media assets.
