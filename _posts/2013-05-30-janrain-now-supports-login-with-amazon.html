For more than a decade, Amazon has helped fuel eCommerce innovation while seeding the market for consumers to shop online.With more than 200 million customers today, people who use Amazon are active, engaged online shoppers.Now, thanks to our partnership with Amazon, these consumers can quickly register and log in to websites using their trusted Amazon identity.Starting today, Janrain customers can easily add Login with Amazon to their website in minutes.Login with Amazon eliminates the need for consumers to remember yet another distinct username and password for each site frequently visited, and our own research indicates some of the uninspiring things people would rather do than think up new passwords.
