Apica is a powerful website and mobile monitoring tool for verifying that your website is available and responding as expected.The integration enables Apica’s alerts to automatically trigger incidents within PagerDuty any time your website becomes unavailable.The Apica integration takes only a few moments to configure.Go to our Apica integration guide for simple instructions.Stackdriver: Intelligent Cloud Monitoring Stackdriver is a SaaS monitoring solution that uses data analytics to help you identify and resolve critical issues with your cloud-based applications.
