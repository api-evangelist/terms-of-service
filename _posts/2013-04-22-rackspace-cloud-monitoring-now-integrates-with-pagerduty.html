PagerDuty is proud to announce an integration with Rackspace Cloud Monitoring, a system that allows you to monitor your critical systems even if they are hosted on a distributed environment. Now that the powers of Rackspace Cloud Monitoring and PagerDuty have been combined, you now have the best of monitoring and alerting systems available, ensuring that your team is notified of critical events with your systems.Getting Started In order to utilize this new integration, you will need a PagerDuty and Rackspace Cloud Monitoring account. Follow these links to sign up for accounts at PagerDuty and Rackspace Cloud Monitoring. To configure your integration you can follow our easy Rackspace Cloud Monitoring Integration Guide.
