The label “Shellshock” initially described a bash security vulnerability, CVE-2014-6271, that is based on bash’s little-known ability to export functions as environment variables.An in-depth explanation can be read at http://seclists.org/oss-sec/2014/q3/650.Several related issues have been discovered that are also being described as part of Shellshock.RedHat’s security blog has a good summary explanation of the issues that fall under the Shellshock label.
