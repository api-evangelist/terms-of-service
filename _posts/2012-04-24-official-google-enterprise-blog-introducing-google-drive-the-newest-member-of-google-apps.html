Today, we’re introducing Google Drive —a place where you can create, share, collaborate, and keep all of your work.Whether you’re drawing up floor plans with a client, creating a presentation with classmates or planning next year’s budget with colleagues, Drive makes it easy to work together.You can upload and access all of your files, including videos, photos, Google Docs, PDFs and beyond.Create and collaborate.Google Docs is built right into Google Drive, so you can work with others in real time on documents, spreadsheets and presentations.
