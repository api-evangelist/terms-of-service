Posted on December 11, 2013 by ilya sabanin Today we launch 2-step login verification, making your accounts more secure than ever before.It adds an extra level of protection against unauthorized access to your account if your password is compromised.Read on for details.Once enabled, two-step verification ensures that your account can not be accessed with just your username and password.Every time you log in, Beanstalk will ask you for a 6-digit verification code in addition to your regular username and password.
