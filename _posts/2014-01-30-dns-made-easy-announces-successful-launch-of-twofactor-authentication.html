DNS Made Easy, the leading provider of enterprise IP Anycast Managed DNS services, announces more than 5,000 users have successfully started using their two-factor authentication, which was released late 2013.This security process involves two stages to verify the identity a user before accessing an account.This new security feature is aimed at protecting our customers from unauthorized access to their private account information.Security is at the forefront of any online service today due to internet-based attacks, which are becoming more frequent as well as more sophisticated.DNS Made Easy joins Apple and Microsoft with its recent release of two-factor support.
